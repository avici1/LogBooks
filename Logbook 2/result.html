1.4 Result

Table below provides the summary of the computerised EDIS that are used to perform a given task. These systems are not integrated and require separate logins

Systems Staff accessibility Task and functionality
Patient Centre System (PCS) Reception staff Patient Registration
Patient Focus Information (PFI) System Clinical techician & reception staff
  • Ordering blood test
  • Viewing blood test results
  • Printing blood tube lables
  • Producing GPs letters
Patient Tracking System (PTS) All staff
  • Patient location and movement
  • Patient care plan
Picture Archiving Communication System (PACS) Doctor &(some) nurse Viewing of x-ray and scan results (radiographically)
Computerised Radiology Information System (CRIS) Reception staff Viewing of x-ray and scan reports
