1.2. Aim and objectives

The overall aim of the research is to do an evaluation on an ED workflow and implementation of the existing information artefacts in supporting the workflow.
More specifically the objectives are:

  1. To describe the characteristics of the ED workflow which consisted of work processes and work practices of the clinicians and non-clinicians, and other organizational elements.
  2. To identify and describe the current information artefacts, both computerised and noncomputerised used to deliver emergency care collaborative work
